Camillo Quattrocchi

AI & Computer Vision Researcher · Ph.D. Student

📍 Catania, Sicily, Italy  ·  FPV-IPLab, University of Catania

I'm a Ph.D. student and researcher at the Image Processing Laboratory (IPLab), University of Catania, working within the First Person Vision (FPV) group led by Prof. Giovanni Maria Farinella. My research sits at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, with a primary focus on egocentric video understanding and temporal action segmentation. I'm passionate about building intelligent systems that perceive and reason about the world through vision — from first-person cameras to autonomous AI agents — and I regularly explore applied LLM engineering and retrieval-augmented generation in side projects.
